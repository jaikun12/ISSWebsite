INFORMATION SYSTEMS SOCIETY

Information Systems Society

The Information Systems Society, also referred to as ISS or UST-ISS, is a not-for-profit organization for the Information Systems community of the University of Santo Tomas

Vision

Guided by the core values of the University and the Information Systems Department, the Information Systems Society commits itself in providing an avenue for growth and social responsiveness for the individuals to harness the abilities and potentials needed for the overall development of each member; to tap the technical and managerial potentials of members to help them become proactive and esteemed members of the society, geared to surpass the challenges of time; to cultivate a custom of comradeship through active involvement in the organization’s events; and to promote a strong sense of ownership for the same vision.

Mission

The Information Systems Society of the University of Santo Tomas aims to be the country’s leading student organization of information systems professionals in pursuit of academic and non-academic par excellence to form and transform society through proactive projects utilizing information technology in its right purpose. To foster and enhance the knowledge and skills of all its members in order to develop them to become competent, compassionate, and committed IS professionals fit to excel in their respective field; and to provide means to bridge the gap between its members and the community outside them in order to fulfill social responsibilities.

Committees




Creatives Committee

Headed by the Vice-President for Creatives, shall be in charge of documenting all the activities of the UST-ISS. This committee shall, also, be in charge of all the advertising prints to be used for various events. They will be responsible in producing event posters and videos required by the officer-in-charge and they accept and deliberate on the articles to be featured in the website, if applicable.

Finance Committee

Headed by the Vice-President for Finance shall be in charge in planning and undertaking fund raising activities of the UST-ISS.

Audit and Logistics Committee

The Audit & Logistics Committee, headed by the Vice-President for Audit shall be in charge of auditing all resources of the organization; preparing the term or semester Audited Finance Statements and submit same to the Executive Board a week before the final examinations; and in keeping all yearly audited financial transactions of the UST-ISS for a period of three (3) years. Moreover, the committee should monitor and provide materials and equipment for use of the organization and must apply for the official locker of the organization.

Community Development Committee

Headed by the Vice-President for Community Development shall be responsible for the planning and preparation of community development activities of the UST-ISS such as, but not limited to the following: outreach programs, helping calamity victims and engaging in medical missions. They shall be in charge of communicating with the different communities, orphanages and other institutions which the UST-ISS may want to help.

Academics Committee

headed by the Vice-President for Academics shall be responsible in conducting co-curricular activities, education and training seminars for the IS students of the University of Santo Tomas, including the preparation of study guides and curricular activites.

Events Committee

Headed by the Vice-for Events shall be responsible for the proposal and implementation of socio-cultural and other related activities of the UST-ISS such as pageants and accustomed social gatherings.

Publicity and Recruitment Committee

headed by the Vice-President for Publicity and Recruitment shall be responsible for disseminating information regarding the activities and other related happenings of the UST-ISS.

Secretariat Committee

The Executive Secretary at Assistant Secretary of the ISS.

External Relations Committee

Headed by the Vice-President for External Relations shall be in-charge in maintaining relationships with organizations of other Universities and of sponsorships for all the activities of the organization.

Sports Committee

Headed by the Sports Coordinators to be appointed by the Executive Board, shall be responsible for the proposal and implementation of sports activities within and outside the organization.

Grievance Committee

Headed by the President and the Grievance Chairman to be appointed by the Executive Board, shall be in charge of handling all the grievances and complaints of the IS students. They will also take charge in monitoring violation of student rights and shall take stand as the legal counsel of the IS students. The Grievance Committee Chairman seats as a voting member of the Executive Board for decision making purposes.

Publicity and Recruitment Committee

headed by the Vice-President for Publicity and Recruitment shall be responsible for disseminating information regarding the activities and other related happenings of the UST-ISS.

Webtech Team

Headed by the Director for Webtech, the webtech team is responsible for developing technology platforms which could help the organization's operations.
